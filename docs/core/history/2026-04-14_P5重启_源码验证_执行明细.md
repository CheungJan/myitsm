- **PB源码SQL**：`Select count(*) From TIT15_EQUIPMENT_RENOVATE`

- **结论**：✅ 逻辑一致，无需调整

#### TIT17_CUST_POS_DAILY（S045）
- **PB源码SQL**：`INSERT INTO TIT17_CUST_POS_DAILY (DAILY_MAINTENANCE_ID, CUST_CD, EID, ITEMCD, STARTDATE, SYSINFO, SOFTINFO, POSUPDDATE, POSINFO, AREA, STATUS='1', TYPFLG='1'/'1','2', UPDATE_TIME)`
- **现有实现**：包含上述所有字段，还额外包含数据库字典中的其他字段（BUSINESS_OPERATION_ID, MAINTENANCEDATE, MAINTENANCETYP, REQUEST_ENGINNER_ID, REQUEST_TIME, SHORT_DESCRIPTION, DETAIL_DESCRIPTION, CREATE_TIME, CREATOR, UPDATOR, USEFLG）
- **结论**：✅ 字段覆盖完整，无需调整

#### TIT23_MAINTENANCE_D2D（S046）
- **PB源码SQL**：`Select count(*) From tit24_maintenance_rv`（关联检查，非TIT23本身）
- **现有实现**：`SELECT * FROM TIT23_MAINTENANCE_D2D WHERE MAINTENANCE_ID=:maintenance_id`
